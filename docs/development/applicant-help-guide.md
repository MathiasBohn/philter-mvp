### Common Questions

**Q: How many references do I need?**
A: We recommend at least 2 references. Most applications include 3-4.

**Q: Can I use my current landlord?**
A: Yes! Current landlords can be excellent references.

**Q: What if I don't have a former landlord?**
A: If this is your first rental, you can use:
- College/university housing office
- Employer or supervisor
- Professional mentor or colleague

**Q: Will the building contact my references automatically?**
A: The building may contact your references during the review process. It's a good idea to give them a heads-up!

**Q: Can I edit references after adding them?**
A: Yes, you can edit or delete references before submitting your application.

A: Provide the information you have. In "Additional Notes," explain that the company is no longer in business.

**Q: How far back should I go?**
A: Minimum 2 years recommended. Some buildings may require 3-5 years.

**Q: What if I'm denied based on the report?**

- Copy of the consumer report
- Summary of Your Rights Under FCRA
- Information about the consumer reporting agency

A: Generally, no. Once given and the report is run, the authorization has served its purpose. Consult legal counsel if needed.

## Common Questions

### General Application Questions

**Q: How long does it take to complete the application?**
A: Most applicants complete it in 1-3 hours, depending on how organized your documents are.

**Q: Can I save and come back later?**
A: Yes! Your application auto-saves. Close your browser and return anytime.

**Q: Can someone help me with my application?**
A: Yes, your broker can help. You can also invite co-applicants to complete their portions.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: You can edit any section before submission. After submission, contact building management to request changes.

**Q: When will I hear back?**
A: Review times vary by building. Typically 1-4 weeks. Check with your broker.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browser should I use?**
A: philter works best on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Can I use my phone or tablet?**
A: Yes! philter is mobile-friendly. However, document uploads may be easier on a computer.

**Q: My data disappeared! What happened?**
A: Check that you're using the same browser and haven't cleared your browser data. If issues persist, contact support.

**Q: Can I use multiple devices?**
A: For the MVP, data is stored per device/browser. Use the same device to avoid data sync issues.

**Solutions:**

- Verify file type (PDF, JPG, PNG accepted)
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and try again

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are complete
- Try refreshing the page
- Check browser console for errors

- Some sections only appear for certain transaction types
- Scroll down - you may have missed it
- Contact your broker for guidance
